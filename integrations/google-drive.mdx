Connecting Google Drive lets HarborOS read your signed contracts straight from a folder you choose and extract their terms — so you don't have to upload each one by hand.

<Note>
Google Drive is available on the **Pro** plan.
</Note>

## Connect

On the **Integrations** screen, click **Connect** on Google Drive and approve access in the Google window that opens. HarborOS requests only the access it needs to read the contract files you point it at.

## Choose a folder

After connecting, pick the folder where your signed contracts live. HarborOS reads the contracts in that folder and extracts each one's terms — the [ARR](/get-started/glossary), dates, and renewal details.

## Confirm what comes in

Extracted contracts don't count toward your numbers until you confirm them — you review what was read and accept it. This is the same confirmation step described in [Judgment is the gate](/concepts/judgment-is-the-gate). You can confirm in bulk, with lower-confidence reads surfaced first.

If your contracts aren't in Drive, you can also upload a PDF directly on the Contracts screen — see the [Quickstart](/get-started/quickstart).
