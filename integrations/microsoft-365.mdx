<Note>
Microsoft OneDrive and SharePoint connections are coming soon.
</Note>

These will let HarborOS read your signed contracts from OneDrive or a SharePoint document library and extract their terms — the same way the [Google Drive](/integrations/google-drive) connection works today.

You can register interest from the **Integrations** screen. Until then, contracts can come in through Google Drive or by uploading a PDF directly — see the [Quickstart](/get-started/quickstart).
