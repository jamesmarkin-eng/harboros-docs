Connecting Salesforce brings your opportunities — stages and close dates — into HarborOS so they can feed your forecast.

## Connect

On the **Integrations** screen, click **Connect** on Salesforce and approve access in the window that opens. There are no keys to copy by hand.

## What happens next

Your Salesforce opportunities sync into **Docking**, HarborOS's review queue. They don't affect your forecast until you promote them — you decide which belong in your pipeline. See [Pipeline review](/workflows/pipeline-review).

You can scope what syncs — minimum ARR, excluding closed opportunities, specific stages or types — and map Salesforce fields to HarborOS. See the [Integrations overview](/integrations/overview) for connection settings shared across every CRM.
