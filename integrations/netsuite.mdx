<Note>
A direct NetSuite connection isn't available yet.
</Note>

HarborOS is built around your contracts as the source of every number, which is independent of your accounting or ERP system. A NetSuite connection would let HarborOS line up against what's recorded there.

If this would be useful for you, let us know from the **Integrations** screen so we can factor it into what we build next. In the meantime, your contracts can come in through [Google Drive](/integrations/google-drive) or direct upload.
