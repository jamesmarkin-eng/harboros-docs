Connecting HubSpot brings your deals — pipelines, stages, and owners — into HarborOS so they can feed your forecast.

## Connect

On the **Integrations** screen, click **Connect** on HubSpot and approve access in the window that opens. That's the whole setup; there are no keys to copy.

## What happens next

Your HubSpot deals sync into **Docking**, HarborOS's review queue. They don't affect your forecast until you promote them — you decide which deals belong in your pipeline. See [Pipeline review](/workflows/pipeline-review) for how that works.

You can scope what syncs — minimum ARR, excluding closed deals, specific stages or types — and map HubSpot fields to HarborOS. See the [Integrations overview](/integrations/overview) for connection settings that apply to every CRM.
